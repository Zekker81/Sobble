Sobble

Sobble is a Water type Pokémon introduced in Generation 8. It is known as the Water Lizard Pokémon.

It is one of the starter Pokémon available in Pokémon Sword & Shield (releasing late 2019) along with Grookey and Scorbunny.

Pokedex data:

National N. 816
Type Water
Species Water Lizard Pokemon

Things we currently know about him:

  • Sobble appears to be based on a chameleon. It has a fin on its head and a curled-up tail.
  • Looks like it's somewhat timid Pokémon that shoots out attacks as it hides itself in the water.
  • The Sword & Shield announcement trailer shows it camouflaging itself in a fountain, but runs off when Grookey approaches.
  • It's the most popular starter from the ones introduced in Gen 8.
